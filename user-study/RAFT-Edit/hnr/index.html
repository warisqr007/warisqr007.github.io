Voice Quality A/B Test

RAFT-Edit static speaker-trait listening study

Headphones required. Please complete this task in a quiet environment using good headphones. You may replay each clip as needed.

  • In each question, listen to both clips and choose which one sounds cleaner/more periodic or noisier/breathier, depending on the question. Focus on voice quality rather than loudness or recording content.
  • Each row has two clips, A and B. One clip is the unedited synthesized reference and the other is the edited version.
  • Choose the clip that best matches the question, then rate your confidence from 1 to 7.
  • Do not judge recording content or speaker identity. Focus only on voice quality.
  • Your responses may be screened for completion and consistency.
